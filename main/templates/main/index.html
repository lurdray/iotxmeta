Tokenomics

With a total of 10 Billion $IotxMeta tokens, the following amounts have been allotted for different purposes:

  • 15% Presale

    A total of $Meme-meta 1.5 billion will be alocated for presale to Iotx ecosystem.

  • 35% Liquidity Pool

    $IotxMeta 3.5 billion will be made available as the supply of liquidity into pools to make it very easy for people to buy and sell.

  • 5% Contest

    500million of the $IotxMeta Token Supply is allocated to the Iotx Meta meme contest.

  • 25% Marketing

    The amount of $IotxMeta to be reserved for promotions and marketing is $IotxMeta 2.5 billion.

  • 15% Partnerships

    The amount of $IotxMeta tokens available for partnerships is $IotxMeta 1.5 billion. This would be used to foster bridges between different communities, projects and established companies that would be involved with $IotxMeta in the future.

  • 5% Advisors

    There will be $IotxMeta 500 million available as rewards for those who provide their tokens for staking.
